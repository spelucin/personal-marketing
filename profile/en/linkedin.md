## About

I started doing technical SEO for enterprise retail. I managed SEO for Plaza Vea, Promart, Oechsle, Real Plaza, and Inkafarma at Intercorp Retail. I optimized Ripley for CyberWow (3.7M sessions in one day). I worked with Scotiabank and Yanbal at Attach Media.

Then I moved to the analytics side. I led digital analytics teams at Notebook Agency and Picnic. I built measurement systems for 30+ clients in SaaS, finance, healthcare, and ecommerce.

Now I'm back to my roots: technical SEO, but focused on Shopify.

Why Shopify? Because I see the same technical errors I saw in enterprise retail: broken implementations, catalog architecture that looks like a maze, pages that don't rank. Small stores deserve the same level of technical expertise as big ones.

**What I do now:**

🔧 **Deep Technical SEO** — Audit of indexing, rendering, speed, and structure. I find the invisible errors killing your traffic and deliver a prioritized roadmap. Most problems get fixed in days, not months.

🏗️ **Catalog Architecture** — I design the structure of collections, navigation, and facets so Google understands your catalog and your customers find what they're looking for. Fewer clicks, more conversion, better rankings.

🤖 **AI Search / GEO** — Optimization for AI-powered search engines. I prepare your store for the future of search.

**Philosophy:** Technical infrastructure over temporary tricks. Systems that scale over quick fixes that break in three months.

If your Shopify store needs to rank higher, attract qualified traffic, and convert that traffic into predictable revenue, let's talk.
